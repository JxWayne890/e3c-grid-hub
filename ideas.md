# E3C Grid Hub — Design Brainstorm

## Context
A front-facing platform for the Grid Worker Movement — connecting contractors, homeowners, D2D sales reps, and 1099 entrepreneurs through a referral flywheel. The brand is rooted in Tesla's 3-6-9 energy philosophy, the E3C collective (Communicate, Collaborate, Connect), and a strong identity around health, wealth, and community.

2. Hard box shadows (no blur) on interactive elements
3. Stamp-style badges for user types and subscription tiers

**Typography System:**

## Selected Design: Dark Luxury Tech — "The Grid as Infrastructure"

This design philosophy best represents the E3C brand: premium, powerful, and built for serious people who are building serious ecosystems. The dark background with gold and electric blue accents directly maps to the Grid Worker Movement's energy — wealth (gold), technology (blue), and depth (dark). It feels like a platform, not a flyer.
